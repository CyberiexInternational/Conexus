====== Bio-Conexus ======
Created Sunday 14 April 2024

The Bio-Conexus theory / religion emerged from a spate of Gloaming related events involving individuals seemingly gifted with the ability to directly experience (or even affect) alternative realities . Encompassing fields as diverse as  Computational Biotech, psychopathology, underground illegal human experimentation and surgery, the term covers everything from enhancing human ability and increasing lifespan to altering humans to create living weapons, computers etc.
